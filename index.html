About Me

Hello! I'm Najmus Sakib Sizan, a passionate technologist specializing in software support and innovative solutions.

I specialize in supporting complex software environments and delivering efficient technical solutions. My current role at BJIT Limited allows me to leverage my expertise in software support. Previously, I served as a Software Support Engineer at Desh Universal (Pvt.) Ltd. and as Tech Coordinator for BuffaloGrid Ltd in Bangladesh.

I hold an MSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Jagannath University, and a BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Green University of Bangladesh, where I also led as the President of the Green University Computer Club (GUCC).

I'm driven by continuous learning and dedicated to applying cutting-edge technology to solve real-world problems. My goal is to make meaningful contributions in the rapidly evolving field of technology and innovation.
