About Me

I am Najmus Sakib Sizan, a Software Support Engineer at BJIT Limited with a strong passion for technology and problem-solving. Throughout my career, I have honed my technical expertise and developed a deep understanding of complex software environments, delivering efficient support and solutions.

Prior to my role at BJIT Limited, I served as a Software Support Engineer at Desh Universal (Pvt.) Ltd. and as Tech Coordinator for BuffaloGrid Ltd in Bangladesh. These experiences have allowed me to refine my skills in technical support and coordination, consistently driving impactful solutions in diverse and challenging settings.

I hold an MSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Jagannath University, as well as a BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Green University of Bangladesh, where I had the honor of serving as the President of the Green University Computer Club (GUCC).

Beyond my professional and academic endeavors, I am actively involved in social initiatives. I have volunteered with Socchota, a non-profit organization focused on environmental sustainability. My commitment to community service aligns with my belief in using technology and innovation for the greater good.

My research interests include the Internet of Things (IoT), Machine Learning, and IoT applications in agriculture. I am particularly focused on leveraging these technologies to innovate in sustainable agriculture, blending my professional skills with my passion for impactful research.

Driven by a commitment to continuous learning and a dedication to applying cutting-edge technology, I aim to contribute meaningfully to the rapidly evolving world of technology and innovation.
